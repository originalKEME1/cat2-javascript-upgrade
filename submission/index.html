Our Story

A Firm Built on Principle

Kemei Advocates & Co. is a full-service corporate law firm headquartered in Nairobi, Kenya. Founded on the conviction that exceptional legal counsel should be accessible, transparent, and decisive, we have grown into a trusted partner for businesses, institutions, and individuals across East Africa and beyond.

Our team brings together deep expertise in corporate law, commercial litigation, real estate, and regulatory compliance — always guided by the highest standards of professional integrity.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule an initial consultation?

You can schedule a consultation by completing the contact form, calling our office directly, or sending an email. We typically respond within one business day.

What should I bring to my first meeting?

Bring any documents relevant to your matter — contracts, correspondence, identification documents, and a brief written summary if helpful.

How are your fees structured?

Our fees vary by matter type. We offer hourly rates, fixed-fee arrangements for defined scope work, and retainer agreements. A clear fee estimate is always provided before any engagement commences.

Do you handle matters outside Kenya?

We regularly advise on cross-border transactions and work with correspondent firms across East Africa and internationally.
